# Burrito

## Ingredients

- 2 tortilla wraps
- Chicken breast
- Frozen vegetables
- Garlic

## Instructions

- Chop and season chicken breast to your liking.
- Saute garlic in oil.
- Pan fry chicken breast.
- Add vegetables.
- Add sauce of your liking to tortilla wraps.
- Spoon chicken and veggie mixture onto the wrap.
- Fold and enjoy!
